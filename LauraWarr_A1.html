About Me

I'm a nerd and an explorer; my main passions in life have been numbers and the outdoors. I've been fortunate enough to have had my work take me from Orthopaedics research in China, to tree planting in northern BC. But if I were to be honest, my dream job would be studying math in the forest — alas, I have yet to find means to make a living doing this.

I aspire to live an active lifestyle; my spare time is typically filled with activities such as rock climbing, running, and swimming. I believe that the best way to spend one's time is outdoors, and I enjoy backpacking and camping whenever I have the opportunity.
